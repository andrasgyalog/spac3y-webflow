Visual Direction — Moodboard

The motif is a single dark crystalline form moving through three states: scattered fragments, a glowing lattice, and a fused monolith. It reads as data becoming physical — raw, disconnected information pulling itself into structure, then into something solid and permanent. The direction deliberately avoids the usual AI clichés: no robots, no holograms, no glowing screens.
